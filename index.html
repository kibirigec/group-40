Frequently asked questions

What is “resilient” infrastructure?

Infrastructure that can handle shocks like floods or heatwaves and still works.

Is SDG 9 only about factories?

No. It includes roads, internet, research, small businesses, and clean technology.

How can students help?

Learn STEM skills, join maker clubs, and support local innovations.

What can you do?

  • Support local businesses adopting clean tech.
  • Speak up for better public transport and internet access.
  • Learn STEM skills and share knowledge with others.
